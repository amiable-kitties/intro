ABOUT

Amiable Kitties is a collection of adorable kitties having fun on the Ethereum blockchain created by artist DaniaSun. Each kitty of the friendly clowder is a unique and amusing character. They are always cute and entertaining.

COMMUNITY

The Amiable Kitties project has been initially focused on creating charming art and building a kind and friendly community. We are here to bring joy and organize a fruitful environment for creativity and pleasant communication. The community of Amiable Kitties is always happy and sweet.

PFP NFTs

RARITY

Some kitties are so rare that others of the NFT community are even scared! The rarity of an NFT is identified by the uniqueness of the character composition. Each Amiable Kitty consists of certain traits. It can be unique traits, or it can be something else. Watch out for the unique ones if you are an attentive collector!
